Projects and Portfolio

  • Boss The Lotto: A lottery-themed game that turns the classic 6-out-of-49 on its head. The player’s job is to find as many good balls as possible while avoiding the 6 bad ones. Meaningful player agency, lottery-style jackpots and high win rate make thisa real first-of-a-kind game.
  • Be The King: Las Vegas meets game show. Focused on the online casino market this game sends the player on a constant up-and-down, putting the player completely in charge of their own game. Initially created as an exclusive this game proved incredibly succesful - Project management, implementation
  • Epic Gems: Taking the ideas of Boss The Lotto I pushed for a game that moves away from the lottery context towards social gaming. By also shifting the focus on speed and ease-of-use on mobile devices we managed to create a game that mathematically similar but attracts a different category of players - Initial concept, maths, implementation
  • Buzzword: Tyring to adapt concepts of a successful gameshow into a real money online game lead to a word-game-bingo mix. The extreme influence of player agency made this game very challenging to simulate, leading to complex mathematical models and experiments - Conception, maths, prototype, paytables, implementation
  • The Link: Perceived as a "noughts and crosses" slots this game went through multiple iterations and paytables until a combintation was found that was both satisfying a slots player while at the same time appealing to more casual players - Conception, maths, paytables
  • Gears of Fortune: Trying to attract a new player segment we experimented with putting trivia into real money games. With Gears of Fortune we managed to combine both skill and luck in a way that allows not-so-skilled players to win regularly while at the same time making sure that operators maintain a house edge even against pub-quiz-champions - Initial concept, maths, prototype, paytables, implementation
  • Wortopia: Multi-player word game based on "Boggle". Played by roughly 1800 players every month.
  • PIXI-Packer: Build tool that streamlines asset resizing and packaging for HTML5 games
  • monte-carlo: Library that provides basic tools to quickly create reliable mathematical simulations of real money games, especially for those with skill elements and player agency.
  • dependency-updater: Open-source script that detects outdated npm dependencies in github projects and opens a PR to update them
